What the agents actually do

When triggered, an orchestrator agent spawns a wave of collector agents that scan the web in parallel. Results come back as structured JSON. The orchestrator ranks everything by signal strength, then spawns a second wave to research the top stories in depth. A synthesis agent writes the final brief from all that output and pushes it to web, email, and Telegram. A memory layer tracks what was covered across runs so the same story never appears twice. The pipeline runs on a schedule or on demand. 9 AM is the default daily run, not a constraint.
